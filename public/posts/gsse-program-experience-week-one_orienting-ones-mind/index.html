The research showed that electronic devices had a significant negative impact upon the students. And based on this conclusion, and rightly so, Professor Stephane banned electronic devices from his lectures. This is something while I found incredibly counter-intuitive for me personally, makes sense for the benefit of the whole class. As our favorite science fiction character, Ambassador Spock, would say: The needs of the many outweigh the needs of the few.
